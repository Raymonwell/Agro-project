Our Value Chain

Our approach is inspired by our determination to improve the agricultural landscape in Africa.

Input Financing

Bridging the financial gap for smallholder farmers through access to quality farm inputs/seedlings and technical support.

Agricultural Technology & Consultancy

Boosting overall farming efficiency by leveraging on the power of technology via our satellite imagery, remote sensing systems & IVRs.

Insurance

Providing adequate farming security from planting to post harvest to ensure maximum productivity.

Aggregation

Ensuring accessibility of conducive storage systems for smallholder farmers after harvest.

Commodity Trading

Exportation of in-demand agro commodities with high level of nutritional and quality standards.

Food Processing

Producing of quality semi-finished Agro products for direct consumption.

Trading Agro Commodities Accross Different Continents.

Handling more than 10 agricultural commodities including cocoa, sesame, cashew, ginger, maize etc. Our warehouse facilities are instrumental in managing post-harvest losses. We take charge of complete warehousing on lease and offer space to different clients for storage of commodites.

Farming, Aggregation and Processing

Smallholder farmers' livelihoods are being improved by providing them with access to finance, smart advisory services and profitable market linkages. We provide critical facilities, infrastructure, and services that enable farmers to expand their markets beyond the farmers market, allowing them to develop and improve local and regional food systems, as well as provide processed produce for the consumers

Agricultural Technology & Consultancy

Leveraging technology for smart farming. Our On-farm technology and data management services help farmers make better decisions, boost efficiency, increase yield and become more sustainable.

Global Exportation of Agro-commodities

In line with our mandate of providing premium markets for smallholder farmers, we have networks of credible off-takers in four continents with the capacity to buy from our onboarded smallholder farmers. We are always open to doing business with more credible off-takers that require high-quality agricultural produce. We have traded over 300,000MT of agro-commodities across four continents. Sesame seeds, cashew nuts, ginger, peanuts, cocoa, hibiscus flower and shea butter are among our specialties.
